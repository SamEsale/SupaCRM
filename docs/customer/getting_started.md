# Getting Started with SupaCRM

This guide describes the customer-facing launch surface that is currently implemented.

## What is available now

- Account authentication with tenant-aware access
- CRM core for companies and contacts
- Sales, quotes, and invoices
- Product catalog with validated image counts
- Subscription-based commercial access
- Operator-assisted tenant onboarding and activation

## First steps

1. Sign in with your tenant-provisioned account.
2. Confirm your tenant is active.
3. Review your onboarding summary.
4. If the tenant is still in trial, start with the starter commercial package.
5. Begin with CRM records, then move into sales, quotes, invoices, and catalog management.

## What is not part of this launch

- Marketing automation
- Social connectors
- Advanced accounting expansion
- Advanced reporting expansion

Those areas are not claimed as launch-ready yet and should be treated as staged roadmap items.
